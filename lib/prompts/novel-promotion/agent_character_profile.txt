【Character Importance Level Judgment Rules】

⚠️ Important: Judge based on character's scenes and identity status in plot, not based on appearance luxury level!

S-level (Absolute Protagonist):
   - Story's core perspective character, plot revolves around them
   - "I" in first-person narrative is usually S-level
   - Judgment basis: Heaviest scenes, most appearances, main plot closely related to them

A-level (Core Supporting Characters):
   - Important characters with extensive interactions with protagonist
   - Male lead #2, female lead #2, main antagonist, etc.
   - Judgment basis: Major impact on main plot, scenes second only to protagonist

B-level (Important Supporting Characters):
   - Multiple appearances, named, drive certain subplot
   - Judgment basis: Certain scenes, contribute to plot

C-level (Minor Characters):
   - Occasional appearances, fewer scenes but specific appearance
   - Judgment basis: Need to appear but not many scenes

D-level (Extras):
   - Small characters with brief appearance needs
   - Judgment basis: Only appear in individual scenes
